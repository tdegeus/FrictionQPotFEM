Convert array of yield strains stored per element [nelem, n]:

  1. Pre-prepend elastic: epsy[e, :] -> [-epsy[e, 0], epsy[e, :]]
  2. Broadcast to be the same for all quadrature points, converting the shape to [nelem, nip, n].
